# Timesheet Billing Manager

A React-based timesheet and billing management application that integrates with Clockify for time tracking data.

## Roadmap

- fixed billings - when adding a fixed billing date, the date is not recorded in the month that is selected. All fixed bid payments appear in the same month and changing the month moves all payments associated to that month. These should belong to the month assigned and not influence other payments. 

Specifically, see 'Enrollment App' there are two payments, one in Feb and the other in Mar. They keep changing months
